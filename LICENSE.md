## 🔧 Common Issues & Fixes

**❌ GUI doesn't appear after execution**
> Confirm your executor is fully injected before running the script. Wait 5–10 seconds after attaching, then execute. If the GUI still doesn't appear, re-inject and try once more.

**❌ Auto Dribble moves in the wrong direction**
> The dribble system reads your camera orientation for direction. Make sure your camera is pointed toward the opposing goal before enabling Auto Dribble. Adjust the "Direction Lock" option in the Combat tab if the issue persists.

**❌ Inf Spin stops working after a few minutes**
> This is caused by a server-side cooldown reset that triggers periodically. Toggle Inf Spin off and back on in the GUI to re-apply the cooldown removal hook.

**❌ Auto Farm stops earning XP**
> The Auto Farm loop requires an active match. If the match ends and a new one begins, the farm loop needs to be re-toggled. Enable the **Auto Rejoin** option in the Farm tab to handle this automatically.

**❌ Hitbox expansion causes me to receive the ball when I don't want to**
> The hitbox size is configurable. Open the Movement tab and reduce the hitbox multiplier from the default 2.5x down to 1.5x for a more controlled reach without unintended ball pickups.

**❌ Mobile crash immediately on execution**
> Reduce Roblox graphics to Level 1 before injecting your executor. High graphics settings combined with executor overhead frequently exceed available RAM on mid-range Android devices.

**❌ Auto Counter isn't blocking tackles**
> Ensure you're running v2.1.0 or later — earlier versions didn't support the Slide Tackle mechanic introduced in the March 2026 update. Update the script and the counter module will work correctly.
